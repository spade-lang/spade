## Added

- Added traits to allow overloading some operators:
    - `Eq`: `==` and `!=`
    - `Ord`: `<`, `<=`, `>=` and `>`
    - `Not`: `!`
    - `And`: `&&`
    - `Or`: `||`
    - `Xor`: `^^`
    - `BitNot`: `~`
    - `BitAnd`: `&`
    - `BitOr`: `|`
    - `BitXor`: `^`
